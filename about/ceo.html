Head of ZeoFlow - Teodor Grigor

Teodor Grigor - also known as Teo, is the Head of ZeoFlow where he oversees all functions of the business including engineering, product and operations. A developer at heart, Teo is known for balancing sharp developer thinking with thoughtful product strategy to create experiences that bring people together.


Teo worked at the ZeoFlow prototype for Android platform since he was 16 years old. In 2016, he made a team with one of his classmates to create android applications for the users around the world - the company was named FrozenCode. Close to the middle of 2017 he left FrozenCode and joined another company (DolphPire) as an Android Developer - where he was the leading and principal android developer and designer, succeeding to create until Aug 2019 more than 10 applications, which can be found on Play Store.


A native of Brasov, Romania - born in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, Teo holds the first place on the MIT App Inventor, an online platform for creating android applications, since he joined the high-school (Sep 2015).

The early life and education of ZeoFlow's CEO

Teodor Grigor attended 'Emil Racovita' College of Natural Sciences (Sep 2015 - July 2019) where he studied computer science. He is attending Northampton University's to learn about computer science and games programming - he should graduate with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Games Programming in July 2022.
